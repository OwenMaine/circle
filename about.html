About Circle City Glass

Craftsmanship, Integrity, and Crystal Clear Results.

Our Story: A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 2005, Circle City Glass started with a simple goal: to provide the Dothan community with reliable, high-quality window and glass services. With over 20 years of combined experience in the industry, our founder saw a need for a company that prioritized both expert craftsmanship and genuine customer care.

Today, we continue to operate on our core values of Integrity, Quality, and Reliability. We believe in doing the job right the first time and building lasting relationships with our clients.
